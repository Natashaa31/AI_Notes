Chapter 1 – Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence is the science and engineering of making intelligent machines, especially intelligent computer programs.

Father of Artificial Intelligence

John McCarthy introduced the term Artificial Intelligence in 1956 and is known as the father of AI.

Turing Test

The Turing Test, proposed by Alan Turing, is used to determine whether a machine can demonstrate intelligent behavior equivalent to a human.

Capabilities of Intelligence

  • Learning
  • Reasoning
  • Problem Solving
  • Perception
  • Language Understanding

Applications of AI

  • Gaming
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Expert Systems
  • Speech Recognition
  • Vision Systems
  • Robotics
