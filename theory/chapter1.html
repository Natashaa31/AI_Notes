Chapter 1 – Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

Definition

Artificial Intelligence is the science and engineering of making intelligent machines especially intelligent computer programs.

Father of AI

John McCarthy introduced the term Artificial Intelligence in 1956.

Turing Test

The Turing Test proposed by Alan Turing determines whether a machine can exhibit human-like intelligence through conversation.

Capabilities of Intelligence

  • Learning
  • Reasoning
  • Problem Solving
  • Perception
  • Language Understanding

Applications of AI

  • Gaming
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Expert Systems
  • Speech Recognition
  • Computer Vision
  • Robotics
